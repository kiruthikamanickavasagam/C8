

Steps to create an account:

1) If you are a new user, then create an account.
2) To create an account, press on create account button.
3) Provide your personal, academic, skill and project related details.
4) Login with your email and password.

Steps to access the dashboard:

1) Choose your interested skill to view the related course.
2) Trending and recommended courses will be mentioned at the bottom of your dashboard.
3) Once completing the course, add your skills.
4) Provide the name and certificate of your course.
5) You Can view and edit your profile in your profile page.


*******Exclusive for final year students*********

1) Placement related details will be provided.
2) Eligible companies will be displayed.
